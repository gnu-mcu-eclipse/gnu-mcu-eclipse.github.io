Deprecation notice

In mid-2019, the ARM toolchain binaries were moved from the GNU MCU Eclipse project to the xPack project. The new home page is:

  • https://xpack.github.io/arm-none-eabi-gcc/

All previous releases are still available in the @gnu-mcu-eclipse scope, but were deprecated and are not recommended for new projects.
